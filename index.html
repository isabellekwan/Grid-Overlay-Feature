View More Info

Click on the dropdown icon on the right side of each category heading to see all related links.

Bookmarked Links

Bookmarked links will be indicated with a filled in star.

Change Category of Link

Links can be recategorized by clicking the category label buttons located at the bottom of each link. Ensure you refresh your page to see the changes.

Helpful Links

"Helpful" and "unhelpful" buttons are available to indicate the usefulness of visited websites. Selected options will appear in blue.

Additional Information

The number of links under each category and the amount of time spent at all the links can be found at the top right corner. As well, the tag attached to each link can be found next to the "helpful" button.
